CRUISE SHIP ACTIVITIES

Come get your cruise on to get your game on. These ships have taken onboard activities to new heights. Get in the fast lane on a thrilling race track, or hang on the largest ropes course at sea. Then slow it down, way down, with a hot stone massage at Mandara Spa. Whether you're setting sail with friends, enjoying a romantic retreat or bringing the whole family along, the smiles come in all sizes with incredible experiences for all ages.

The Biggest Thrills at Sea

THRILLPLEX

Cruise into adrenaline-pumping activities in our ThrillPlex activity zones that'll keep you on the edge of your seat. Zoom around one of the largest race tracks at sea. Discover larger-than-life adventures during an exciting game of augmented reality laser tag. Hang with friends on the largest ropes course at sea and then whoosh over the side of the ship on Ocean Loops in the Aqua Park. Or dive into a world unlike any other in the Galaxy Pavilion and immerse yourself in a virtual gaming wonderland. Get in the zone for the biggest thrills at sea.

THE WATERFRONT


Exclusively on Breakaway and Breakaway Plus class ships, our revolutionary quarter-mile oceanfront promenade is designed to connect you with the sea. Discover a new restaurant, or simply watch the sun set with a mojito in your hands.


Relax & Rejuvenate

SELF-CARE AT SEA

Relax & Rejuvenate at the spa: When you want some "me" time, look no further than Mandara Spa®. Relax with a hot-stone massage, one of over 50 specialty treatments offered. Let our pampering professionals soothe and rejuvenate you with facials, massages, acupuncture and a full menu of services.


Stay Active on your Cruise: Even though you're going on a cruise, you don't need to skip your daily exercise. In fact, a cruise holiday is an ideal time to shake up your fitness routine by trying a new sport or incorporating an entirely new activity into the mix.
